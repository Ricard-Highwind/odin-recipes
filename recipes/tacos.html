Tacos

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 8 corn or flour tortillas
  • Lettuce, finely chopped
  • Shredded Mexican cheese blend or cheddar cheese
  • Tomatoes chopped
  • Chopped red onions

Directions

  1. Cook the meat, draining fat
  2. Add spices ad sauces
  3. Assemble and serve
