Deep Dish Pizza

Ingredients

  • 3 and 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup yellow cornmeal
  • 1 and 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 2 and 1/4 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 and 1/4 cups slightly warm water
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • Olive oil
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 small onion, grated
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 28 ounces crushed tomatoes
  • 1/4 tsp granulated sugar
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 4 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled

Directions

  1. Combine flour, cornmeal, salt, sugar, and yeast in a bowl and mix to create dough
  2. Form dough into ball, coat in oil, let rise
  3. Flour a work surface, knead dough to shape
  4. Sauce crust, assemble pizza
  5. Bake
