Gyros

Description

Gyros are a great mediterranean dish using a mix of beef and lamb meet and a zesty cucumber sauce. The meat, various veggies, and sauce are all placed on a piece of pita bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground lamb
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, small
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 tsp Aleppo pepper (sub paprika and a dash of cayenne)
  • 1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 8 pieces pita bread, warmed
  • 1 cup tzatziki sauce
  • 3 campari or roma tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 romaine heart, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese crumbles, optional
  • Hot sauce, optional

Instructions

  1. Make gyro meat
  2. Bake meat
  3. Slice meat
  4. Brown meat
  5. Assemble Gyros
